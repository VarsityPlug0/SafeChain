Welcome to CryptoVault!

Here's a step-by-step guide to get you started.

First, you'll need to register. Go to the registration page and fill in your details. If you have a referral code, be sure to enter it to get a bonus!

Once you're logged in, head over to your wallet and click "Deposit Funds." You can add money using various payment methods. Your funds will appear in your "Available Balance" once approved.

Go to the investment tiers page to see the available plans. Choose one that suits you and click "Invest Now."

Your dashboard gives you a complete overview of your investments, earnings, and referrals. You can track your progress here.
